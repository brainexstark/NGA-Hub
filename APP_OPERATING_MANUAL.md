# NGA Hub: High-Performance Operating Manual
## STARK-B Legacy Network Protocols (v1.0)

Welcome to the **NGA Hub**, a next-generation platform for high-performance learning, creativity, and community synchronization. This manual details the procedures for operating every synchronized node in the system.

---

### 1. Initial Synchronization (Account & Personalization)
*   **Account Creation**: Initialize your node via `Sign Up`. Provide your username, secure email, and a unique security token (password).
*   **Age-Group Protocol**: Select your sector (`under-10`, `10-16`, or `16-plus`). The environment background and content filters will automatically synchronize to match your demographic.
*   **Identity Preview**: Visit `Settings` to update your community display name and paste a custom avatar URL. The identity preview node reacts instantly to visual asset links.

### 2. The Communication Hub (Chating & Status)
*   **Encrypted Transmissions**: Access the `Chating` node from the dashboard or sidebar.
*   **WhatsApp-Inspired Threading**:
    *   **Double Ticks**: Confirm your transmission has been synchronized and read.
    *   **Typing...**: View active node processing from the Intelligence Core.
    *   **Emojis**: Use emojis freely across all transmissions for enhanced expressiveness.
*   **Status Nodes**: Navigate to the `Status` tab to view recent network synchronizations. Click `My Status` to broadcast a temporary update.
*   **Persona Sync**: Click the `User+` icon in the New Chat dialog to synchronize a new identity into your network lineage by providing a name and avatar URL.

### 3. Discovery Matrix (HomeTon & Super Feed)
*   **Dashboard Discovery**: The `HomeTon` node features localized data including your detected city node and active story clusters.
*   **Legacy Shorts**: Swipe or click through high-performance short-form broadcasts (SpaceX missions, Mark Rober engineering, etc.).
*   **Internal Node Discovery**: Click on social icons (X, Reddit, GitHub) to launch platforms directly within the hub using high-performance internal viewers.
*   **Protocol Override**: If an external site restricts synchronization, use the `Override` button in the viewer header to launch an external link.

### 4. Educational Missions (Learning Hub)
*   **Academic Nodes**: Enter the `Learning Hub` to access 30-minute high-performance sessions across Math, Science, ICT, and more.
*   **NEUROSYN AI Coach**: Click the AI Coach button to synchronize your academic concerns with the core intelligence for immediate performance advice.
*   **Heartbeat Logging**: Completing a mission automatically synchronizes a completion node to your community heartbeat and increments your performance metrics.

### 5. Creation Protocols (Posts & Media)
*   **Broadcast Node**: Use `New Post` to send permanent media to the community feed. Support for image, video, reel, and story formats is active.
*   **Video Bank (Superdatabase)**:
    *   **URL Deposit**: Sync external videos by pasting a link.
    *   **Local Node Deposit**: Toggle to `Local Node` to synchronize media assets directly from your hardware storage.
*   **Live Stream**: Initialize a `Live Broadcast` to activate your hardware camera and stream missions to the community in real-time. Use the `Record` protocol to capture sessions to memory.

### 6. System Governance (Security & Limits)
*   **30-Minute Session Limit**: Your entertainment protocol is synchronized to 1800 seconds (30 minutes). Once expired, you must synchronize with an educational node to restore normalcy.
*   **Security Node**: Visit the `Security` page for real-time AI-generated threat intelligence briefings and safety recommendations.
*   **Lockdown Protocol**: In the event of a security breach, the STARK-B protocol will suspend your node. Only authorized administrators can restore full system workability.

### 7. Hardware Synchronization (PWA Installation)
*   **Native Installation**: Visit the `Install Hub`. Click `Execute Installation` to synchronize NGA Hub directly to your device hardware for high-performance access and offline caching.
